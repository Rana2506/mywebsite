INNOVATING THE FUTURE OF SAFETY
Heinrich Safety Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a forward-thinking safety solutions provider established in 2025, committed to protecting lives through innovation, precision, and adherence to global safety standards. Corporate Office at canal street road, Shreebhumi, Laketown, Kolkata-70048, we specialize in designing, supplying, and implementing high-quality Lifeline Safety Systems, Fall Arrest Systems, and Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) across various industrial and commercial sectors.

Our company draws its core inspiration from the Heinrich Safety Pyramid, a foundational principle in occupational safety that underscores the importance of proactive risk management and incident prevention. At Heinrich Safety Solutions, we believe that every near miss is a warning, every hazard is preventable, and every life matters. This philosophy shapes our approach to safety — focusing on early interventions and rigorous system reliability to prevent serious incidents before they happen.
Our Mission
Our mission is simple yet powerful: To create safer workplaces by delivering advanced, reliable, and customized safety solutions tailored to meet the unique challenges of every client. We partner with businesses across sectors to help them achieve regulatory compliance, operational efficiency, and, most importantly, peace of mind when it comes to worker safety.
What We Offer
  • Lifeline Safety Systems – Engineered solutions for rooftop, vertical, and horizontal fall protection, meeting international safety norms.
  • Fall Arrest Systems – High-performance, certified systems designed to arrest falls effectively and minimize injury risk.
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) – Durable, ergonomic, and compliant protective gear tailored for industrial use.
Every product we offer undergoes strict quality checks and is backed by a team of experienced professionals who ensure seamless integration, installation, and after-sales support.
Our Promise
At Heinrich Safety Solutions Pvt. Ltd., we don’t just deliver equipment — we deliver trust. Our tagline, "Your Safety, Our Priority", is more than a statement — it's a responsibility we uphold in every project, every product, and every partnership.

Join us in building safer workplaces, where every worker returns home safely — every day.
