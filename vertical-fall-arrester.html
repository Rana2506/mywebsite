Vertical Fall Arrester

Advanced safety device for unrestricted vertical movement with instant fall protection

Premium Quality
Smart Design

The Vertical Fall Arrester is a key safety device designed for use on ladders, towers, or vertical structures. It travels smoothly along a stainless steel wire rope, allowing unrestricted vertical movement while instantly locking in the event of a fall. Built for reliability, it ensures continuous fall protection in high-risk vertical access areas, making it an essential component for worker safety in elevated work environments.

Standard: EN 795 : 2012
Material: AISI 304
Working Load: 12 kN
Breaking Strength: 25 kN
Component Code: HVLC-08
