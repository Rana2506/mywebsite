Traveler / Glider

Allows smooth, continuous movement along horizontal lifeline while keeping the user securely connected

Premium Quality
Smart Design

The traveler (or glider) allows smooth, continuous movement along a horizontal lifeline while keeping the user securely connected. Made from durable stainless steel, it supports 8 mm cable diameters and is designed to pass over intermediate anchors without disconnecting. Fully compliant with EN 795:2012, it enhances safety and mobility in fall protection systems.

Standard: EN 795 : 2012
Material: AISI 304 / 316
Allowable Working Load: 12 kN
Breaking Strength: 25 kN
Component Code: HHLC-10
