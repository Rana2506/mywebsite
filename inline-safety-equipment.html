Inline Safety Equipment

We offer a comprehensive selection of inline safety equipment to address emergencies and maintain a safe workplace.

Attachment Plate

Provides a secure mounting point for safety systems and equipment.

Clevis Connector

Connects components in safety lines, allowing flexible and strong joints.

Crimping Body and Connector

Ensures secure cable terminations for reliable inline safety systems.

End Anchor

Acts as a fixed point for securing safety lines and equipment ends.

Shock Absorber Spring

Reduces impact forces in the event of a fall, enhancing user safety.

Tensioner Assembly

Maintains proper cable tension for effective inline safety operation.

Tensioner Body

Core component of the tensioning system for safety cables.
